Flex helpers

There are a couple flex helpers that make layout a breeze.

.flex-stack

.flex-stack is a great starting place most of the time. It provides a flex container, and sizes direct children to be flex: 1 by default. Each direct child is stacked on mobile. You may use any of the classe on this page to further customize .flex-stack.
